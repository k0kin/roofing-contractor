    Brighton

    is a city in the Detroit metropolitan area[4] in southeastern Livingston County, Michigan, United States. As of the 2020 census, the city population was 7,446.[5] Brighton forms part of the South Lyon-Howell-Brighton Urban Area. It is one of two incorporated cities in Livingston County, and incorporates land that was part of Brighton, Green Oak and Genoa townships.

    About Brighton, Michigan

    Brighton was established in 1832. It was incorporated as a village in 1867 and as a city in 1928.
